Key Insights from the Dashboard

  • Blood Pressure: Elevated blood pressure strongly correlates with heart disease.
  • Cholesterol: High cholesterol levels significantly increase cardiovascular risk.
  • Diabetes: Diabetic patients are more prone to heart-related complications.
